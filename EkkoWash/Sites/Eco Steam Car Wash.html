What is the Capital investment to start a Car Wash franchisee with Steam Car Wash?

Bare minimum capital investment!

if you are looking to open a Steam Car Wash Car Wash and Detailing outlet, the total one-time investment including franchisee-fee will be just 5 Lakhs.

Alternately, if you looking to become a Mobile franchisee, the advantage is that, no shop/office space is required (Save huge on rental charges every month), additionally, since it operates using steam, there is no water discharge spoiling the wash area. This would mean, you can operate your mobile units by having tie-ups with malls / corporate parking / apartment parking with ease.

Below are the vehicle and machine requirements of a one self sufficient unit of Steam Car Wash. One mobile unit / Van will cost you roughly INR 8.50 Lakhs

* One Maruti Omni Van (roughly 2.7 Lakhs)

* One Steam Machine (roughly 3.5 Lakhs)

* One German High end Vacuum cleaner

* One German Upholstery Cleaner

* One Japanese Portable Generator

* Includes Franchisee fee of 1.2 Lakhs as well.

What skill set is required to run this business?

Passion for customer service! Other than that, no special skills required for running this business. Yes, a detailed training on how to operate the steam machine, vacuum and Upholstery cleaner, how to use the accessories will also be provided.

What is the likely monthly income in this business?

Minimum profit of 50K-60K INR per month (Per mobile unit / Van) if you are serving individual customers.
And since in most areas, we are routing leads from corporate offices or malls, for services in their parking lot, the profit can go beyond 1 Lakh INR per month per unit.
You can multiply your profit by investing on more number of mobile units as you earn more confidence, it's just as simple as that.
