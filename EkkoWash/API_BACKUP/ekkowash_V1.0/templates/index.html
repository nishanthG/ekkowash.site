Why we want to go EcoFriendly.

Are we on the verge of running out of water?

Water scarcity is the lack of freshwater resources to meet water. Already, due to many reasons- some due to nature, many due to human activity - we are on the brink of water crisis!

When you book a Ekko Wash service you can save upto 15 lts of water - that, which can be used for fulfilling basic needs for many people across our country. Water saving is the need of the hour and we at Ekko Wash are working hard to do our bit to help this cause. You can too.

We are one of the water savers in the car cleaning industries; we are encouraging you to save water and save lives too.
